Commands: 
Get a quick exercise: quick x (where x is a positive integer)
Generate a specific list of exercises: generate PARAM1 PARAM2 ... x 
PARAM stands for a specific requirement you want your exercise to have
Parameters available for Generate:
gym (filters for exercises that need gym equipment)
static (filters for static exercises)
easy/medium/hard (filters for exercises with a specific diffculty)
